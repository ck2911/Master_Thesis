# Evidence To Claim

| Claim | Evidence Used | Boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ECB surprises provide the policy-news shock | High-frequency announcement surprises | Supports response analysis, not welfare claims |
| Housing finance responds persistently | House-purchase lending and mortgage-flow measures | Housing finance, not exact house prices |
| Compensation-linked response is weaker | Wage tracker and wage-bill measures | Wage pressure, not total compensation |
| Financial intermediation matters | Lending spreads, credit variables, and banking indicators | Timing evidence, not structural bank mediation |
| The main result is not sample-specific | Alternative shocks, clean events, rolling estimates, and exclusions | Directional robustness, not exact equality of magnitudes |
| The interpretation is bounded | Claim-boundary and identification notes | Reduced-form transmission only |
