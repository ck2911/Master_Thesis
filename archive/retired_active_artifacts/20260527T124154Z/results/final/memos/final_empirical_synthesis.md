# Final Empirical Synthesis

The identification rebuild moves the thesis from quarterly ECB-assets IV toward monthly high-frequency ECB monetary-policy surprises.

The monthly first-stage tournament does not accept a hard-IV treatment bridge. The active empirical layer is therefore reduced-form dynamic transmission from exogenous policy-surprise shocks.

Housing and compensation are not interpolated into monthly data. The final comparison uses accepted official monthly housing-finance and real negotiated-wage-pressure proxies reported in `results/final/diagnostics/` and the canonical notebook, with exact structural housing-price, compensation, welfare, and redistribution magnitudes outside the claim boundary.
